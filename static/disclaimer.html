Disclaimer & Educational Use

Last updated: May 2026

⚠️ Important: AmmoniteID is an educational tool for hobbyists and enthusiasts. It is NOT a professional identification service and should not be relied upon for scientific, commercial, or legal purposes.

1. Educational Purpose Only

AmmoniteID uses artificial intelligence to suggest fossil identifications based on images you provide. These suggestions are for educational and recreational use only. The app is designed to:

  • Help you learn about fossils you find
  • Suggest possible families and genera based on image analysis
  • Serve as a reference guide while fossil hunting
  • Encourage curiosity about paleontology

2. AI Limitations

Artificial intelligence is not infallible. The app may:

  • Misidentify specimens based on lighting, angle, or photo quality
  • Confuse similar-looking fossils or non-fossils
  • Suggest identifications with varying accuracy levels
  • Fail to identify unusual or rare specimens

Always verify results with field guides, scientific literature, museum resources, or a qualified paleontologist before drawing conclusions.

3. Not Professional Appraisal

AmmoniteID does NOT provide:

  • Professional fossil authentication
  • Scientific classification or taxonomy
  • Monetary valuation or appraisal
  • Expert geological assessment
  • Field guidance for professional paleontology

For professional identification, consult a museum, university paleontology department, or certified paleontologist.

4. Expert Review Feature

AmmoniteID offers an optional expert review where you can submit fossils for feedback. This feature:

  • Provides guidance from experienced hobbyists or educators
  • Is NOT a substitute for professional paleontological assessment
  • Offers suggestions for reference only
  • Does not constitute scientific authentication

Expert review responses are provided on a best-effort basis and should be verified independently.

5. Liability Disclaimer

We are NOT liable for:

  • Incorrect identifications or misidentifications

  • Harm caused by decisions made based on the app's suggestions

You use AmmoniteID entirely at your own risk.

6. Responsible Use

When using AmmoniteID, we recommend you:

  • Treat suggestions as starting points, not conclusions
  • Cross-reference results with multiple field guides
  • Consult qualified experts for valuable or unusual finds
  • Document your own observations (notes, measurements, location)
  • Build your knowledge of fossil morphology independently
  • Respect geological sites and legal restrictions on fossil collection
✓ Best practice: Combine AmmoniteID with your own research, field guides, and conversations with fossil enthusiasts to build genuine paleontological knowledge.
